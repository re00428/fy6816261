Abstract

            Most speakers used today in your house, cinemas, lecture theatres and night clubs use simple designs where a speaker driver is mounted in a sealed box or with basic ports at the back of the cabinet. What I will be discussing a special type of speaker called Transmission lines. These recycle the sound energy from the rear facing side of the speaker and direct it along a long (normally folded) pathway in the speaker enclosure thus changing the path difference of the sound waves (this is difference in distance a wave travels to a point compared to another). When tuned allows for a much better control of the energy produced by the speaker and improving the fidelity of the sound or more specifically the bass frequency responsiveness (how well a speaker reproduces bass frequencies).
